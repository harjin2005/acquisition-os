# Growth Capability (incl. Sales seed)

**Purpose:** demand generation for a community-driven wedge (VERIFIED channel, DOC-110 §3) with credibility the strategy can survive — our content competes on published accuracy, so it must be true.

**Responsibilities:** content calendar aligned to launch narrative (accuracy reports, migration guides, metro market notes) · SEO program (switch-intent keywords: "PropStream export", "BatchLeads alternative" — adapter-defined) · blog + LinkedIn drafting in house voice · email list + nurture · community presence plan (masterminds, podcasts, REIAs — adapter list) · brand kit · analytics (traffic→demo→design-partner funnel).
**Sales sub-capability (L1):** design-partner CRM (lightweight: pipeline stages, next-step commitments — in memory, not a new tool until >50 opportunities) · prospect research briefs (team size, metro, deal history from public signals) · outreach drafts (founder sends — L1 permanently until brand risk is understood) · qualification against ICP/anti-ICP (DOC-120 §2, hard-coded in the adapter) · forecast honesty: commit/likely/possible with evidence.
**Inputs:** positioning docs, research briefs, funnel analytics. **Outputs:** calendar, drafts, briefs, pipeline reports. **Memory writes:** every prospect interaction, every published piece + performance.
**Approval rules:** *nothing publishes or sends without human action* (L1 ceiling until further ADR). Accuracy-claim content additionally requires the eval-report source linked (our marketing quotes our own evals or it doesn't ship).
**Decision boundaries:** no fake-review/astroturf content ever (DOC-120 §10 clean-hands policy) · no outreach to anti-ICP · no engagement-bait contradicting the decision-quality brand.
**Failure modes:** content mill drift → quality bar = would a skeptical operator save this?; vanity metrics → funnel reports end at design-partner conversations, not impressions.
**KPIs:** qualified demo requests/month · design-partner pipeline adds · content→demo attribution · publish cadence kept.
