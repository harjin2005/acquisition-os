# Finance Capability

**Purpose:** the founder always knows runway, burn, margins, and unit economics — from source systems, not vibes. Deliberately L0→L1: finance automation errors are expensive; drafting and assembly are the win, judgment stays human (+ accountant).

**Responsibilities:** monthly close pack (P&L, burn, runway, MRR/ARR when revenue exists, cohort view later) assembled from Stripe/bank/accounting exports · **per-org COGS tracking** (the DOC-130 dashboard is the source; finance joins it to revenue — gross margin per DOC-110 kill criteria is a *monthly board-level number*) · 12-month rolling forecast with explicit assumptions (each labeled with confidence) · budget vs actual by category · pricing-model bookkeeping (ADR-011 wallet accounting) · investor reporting pack (FounderOS assembles, finance supplies).
**Inputs:** Stripe, bank feeds, accounting exports, COGS dashboard, headcount plan. **Outputs:** close pack, forecast, runway alerts. **Memory writes:** every close pack + assumptions (forecast accuracy becomes reviewable — we eval our own forecasting like we eval our agents).
**Approval rules:** L1 — no payments, no transfers, no filings; assembly and variance analysis only. Runway <9 months = automatic top-slot alert.
**Failure modes:** silent categorization drift → chart-of-accounts SOP + monthly reconciliation checklist; forecast theater → assumptions list is mandatory and scored quarterly against actuals.
**KPIs:** close pack by day 5 · forecast error trend · founder hours on assembly (→0) · zero surprise cash events.
